A pahar (ਪਹਿਰ) is a three-hour watch — eight in a full day, four in the night, which runs sunset to sunrise. Amritvela is the last pahar of the night: the Sikh Rehat Maryada (Art. 4) places it about three hours before dawn. Rehraas Sahib is read at sunset and Kirtan Sohila before sleeping. Times shown are the fixed feed times. The subscribable nitnem.ics always uses the fixed global times 03:30 · 18:30 · 21:00.
